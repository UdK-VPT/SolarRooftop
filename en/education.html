Education

The Rooftop Building is used for classes which are thematically related to the experimental building (questions about building physics, indoor climate, heating, cooling, and ventilation, solar energy use in buildings, building automation, Smart Buildings, etc.) for students of the UdK Berlin and the TU Berlin.

The part of the building that was originally planned at a living room is now used as a seminar room.
