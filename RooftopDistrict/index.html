Virtual Rooftop Building

Please, explore two variants of the Rooftop Building on top of an old town house in the center of Berlin.














Navigation instructions

  • Use your arrow keys of your keyboard for changing your location.
  • Use your touchpad ore mouse for adapting your view direction.
  • 1st: navigate into the back yard of the yellow town house.
  • 2nd: go close to the glass elevator in the middle of the back yard and call the lift by pressing the L key.
  • 3rd: go into the elevator, move close to the button and move up by pressing the L key again.
  • 4th: Leave the elevator and explore with the same navigation technique both Rooftop Buildings.
  • A hint: within both buildings you will find different interactive building components: e.g., you can adapt the position of the PV facade elements by pressing the respective keys which are displayed if your are close to them.
